Let's Predict now?

Teach me,Senpai!

Follow the steps belows and viola!, you are just one click away from your results.

STEP 1

Collect your CGPA's from your report card or college portal. This should be a number between 1.0 and 10.0 .

STEP 2

Enter CGPA's in correct blanks, Be careful while entering if an invalid CGPA is entered ,you have to do it again.

STEP 3

Leave the fields as it is for the sessions you want your score to be predicted for.

STEP 4

Press submit to know your estimated scores.

FINISH

That's it, you are just one click away from knowing your scores.
